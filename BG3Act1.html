Baldur's Gate 3 - Act 1

Prologue: The Nautiloid

After the opening cutscene, you wake up bloodstained and filthy. The nautiloid is burning, and there's a tadpole pool in front of you. You can interact with it, but all it does is knock you over. There are a few points of interest in this area - a mindflayer corpse, usually with a gem as loot; a cartilaginous chest with some random loot in a raised area behind the corpse; an onyx and another cartilaginous chest on the upper level right behind the point where you wake up. Pick these up and make your way to the next circular chamber by going through the sphincter. Yeah.

>When you gain control, go up the ramp to the southeast. You will go to an upper platform where you can find a Cartilaginous Chest as well as Onyx on the table before the chest. If you can't see them, press the Left Alt key to highlight the objects.

Go past the Sphincter or check your map and go west. Interact with the Neural Apparatus in the center of the room to ascend to the upper platform. Up here you'll find Myrnath in a chair. You can pass a Perception check to successfully inspect Myrnath. Ask if you are talking to a man or a brain for an Arcana check. Passing it reveals you are talking to an intellect devourer, a mind flayer minion.

You have a few options:

Check Type Difficulty To Clear Success Failure
Investigation 10 Unlocks Medicine Check Fail to identify condition
Strength 10 Unlocks Dexterity Check Can't extract brain
Dexterity 10 Unlocks Dexterity Check Can't extract brain

All brain extractions will lead to a Dexterity check:

Check Type Difficulty To Clear Success Failure
Dexterity 15 Gain Us as an ally Us runs away

Destroying the brain is the default option if you fail, though you can always leave the body. A successful extraction but a failed Dexterity check will have "Us" run away. Us can join your party without the second Dexterity check. You can also leave it alone or fight it.

Return to the bottom and head through the western exit. A scene will play where you encounter Lae'Zel, a Fighter who joins your party.

The Temple of Lathander and Creche Y'llek

Once you have completed the bulk of Act 1, you take the mountain pass and you come to the Trielta Crags. The Trielta Crags waypoint is North NorthWest of the entry point, and you cant miss it if you follow the road.The roa forks at the waypoint. If you take the east fork and circle back south, you meet the trader Lady Esther. The Lady Esther give you the Githyank Egg quest, and also stocks healing potions. Her best item is the Graceful Cloth, a +2 Dexterity Robe which also increases your jump distance - ideal for barbarians, and maybe sorcerers. I used it on Karlach along with the Bracers of Defence - and that took her armor class to 17.

Further north, the road is mined with repulsor mines which knock you back but don't do much damage. At the end of the road are undead - two Death Shepherds in heavy armor, 4 ghouls and a ghast. The ghould have a stinking cloud aura that makes you nauseous. Nausea makes you unable to take actions, so it is a bit of a pain for your melee warriors. Also, the Death Shepherds have the annoying tendency of reviving killed undeads, inclding themselves, so killing them early, and in the same turn is advisable.
